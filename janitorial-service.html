Janitorial Services

A tidy, clean office or business environment gives a good impression to clients. Sometimes, employees get too busy to help with emptying trash and tidying up on a regular basis, and it can reflect on the overall image of the company.

RANS Facility Management provides professional janitorial services tailored to your specific needs. Our dedicated team ensures a spotless workplace that promotes a healthier, safer, and more productive environment.

  • Improves employee morale and productivity
  • Enhances your brand image and reputation
  • Reduces the risk of illness and workplace accidents
  • Customized plans to suit your company's needs and budget

Our Janitorial Services Include

  • Daily/Weekly/Monthly Office Cleaning
  • Trash Removal and Eco-friendly Disposal
  • Restroom Cleaning and Deep Sanitization
  • Break Room and Kitchenette Cleaning
  • Floor Sweeping, Mopping, and Polishing
  • Carpet Vacuuming and Spot Treatment
  • Window, Glass, and Mirror Cleaning
  • Dusting Desks, Furniture, and Fixtures
  • Supply Management (Tissue, Soap, Sanitizers)
  • Customized Cleaning Schedules and Emergency Cleaning
