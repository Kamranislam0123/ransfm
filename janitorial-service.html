Janitorial Services

A tidy, clean office or business environment gives a good impression to clients. Sometimes, employees get too busy to help with emptying trash and tidying up on a regular basis, and it can reflect on the overall image of the company.

RANS Facility Management provides Janitorial services that include regular cleaning & maintenance of your office. We ensure that your environment remains fresh, welcoming, and professional at all times.

Our Janitorial Services Include

  • Daily/Weekly/Monthly Office Cleaning
  • Trash Removal and Disposal
  • Restroom Cleaning and Sanitization
  • Break Room and Kitchenette Cleaning
  • Floor Sweeping, Mopping, and Polishing
  • Window and Glass Cleaning
  • Supply Management (Tissue, Soap, Sanitizers)
  • Customized Cleaning Schedules

Why Choose Our Janitorial Services?

Professional Staff

Flexible Scheduling

Eco-Friendly Products
