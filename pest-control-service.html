Pest Control Services

Pest control is the regulation or management of species defined as pests, a member of the animal kingdom that impacts adversely on human activities. The response depends on the importance of the damage done and ranges from tolerance, through deterrence and management, to complete eradication.

In homes and urban environments, pests like rodents, birds, and insects share habitats with humans, spoiling possessions and spreading disease. RANS Facility Management offers customized pest control strategies, ensuring safe, efficient, and eco-friendly solutions to protect your environment.

Why Pest Control is Important

Effective pest management protects your health, property, and environment.

Safeguard Property

Prevent costly damage caused by termites, rodents, and insects to buildings and belongings.

Protect Health

Reduce disease risks from pests like mosquitoes, cockroaches, and rats.

Eco-Friendly Methods

We use sustainable, environmentally conscious pest control solutions wherever possible.

Our Pest Management Approach

Integrated strategies tailored to your environment for safe and lasting results.

Step 1
Exclusion

Sealing entry points to prevent pests from invading your space.

Step 2
Repulsion

Using repellents to drive pests away from key areas.

Step 3
Physical Removal

Safe capture and removal of pests from premises.

Step 4
Biological Control

Applying nature-based solutions like sterilization or predator species to control pest populations.
