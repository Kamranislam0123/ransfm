Total Facility Management

Now a day, many organizations are outsourcing all of their non-core services. This is known as Total Facilities Management (TFM). TFM encompasses all the services required for an organization to operate day-to-day, ensuring operational efficiency, reduced overheads, and enhanced focus on core business goals.

Services Included in TFM

  • Cleaning and sanitation
  • Security services and access control
  • Hygiene and pest control
  • Lift operation and maintenance
  • Power generator operation and servicing
  • Mechanical and electrical (M&E) maintenance
  • Waste management and recycling
  • Facility helpdesk and support

Why Organizations Choose TFM?

  • Comprehensive Coverage: All services managed under one contract for simplified coordination.
  • Cost-Effective Operations: Reduces expenses related to manpower, equipment, and administrative overhead.
  • Expertise Across Domains: Access to trained professionals across a wide range of facility services.
  • Improved Workplace Efficiency: Ensures the environment remains clean, safe, and functional at all times.
  • Scalable and Flexible: Services can be tailored and scaled to fit evolving business needs.
