Recruitment Policy

Executive Level

  • Recruitment on an "as needed" basis by Admin Department.
  • Two-phase interview: Phase 1 by Managing Director, Phase 2 by Chairman.
  • Criteria: Academic excellence, communication, teamwork, aptitude, and ability to handle pressure.

General Maintenance Personnel

  • Also recruited on an "as needed" basis with strict verification and references.
  • Three-phase interview: Written test → Operation Director Interview → Managing Director Interview.

Entry Requirements:

  • Site Manager: Postgraduate, 2 years of industry experience
  • Site Coordinator: Graduate/HSC, 2 years of industry experience
  • Sr. Supervisor: HSC, 2 years experience, trained by RANS
  • Supervisor: HSC, 1 year experience, trained by RANS
  • Sr. Security Guard: Age 22+, Class 8 pass, 1 year experience, trained by RANS
  • Security Guard: Age 18+, Class 8 pass, trained by RANS
  • Cleaners: Age 18+, Class 8 pass, trained by RANS
  • Gardeners: Class 8 pass, 2 years gardening experience

All individuals undergo character verification. Personal dossiers are maintained, recording leave, attendance, and performance data.

Training Procedures

Janitorial & TFM Services

Training includes both theoretical and practical lessons, conducted in a fully equipped training center with a full-time Training Manager.

Training Phases:

  • Day 01: Company orientation, quality standards, equipment knowledge, dust control mop, basic mopping, glass/toilet cleaning, vacuum machine
  • Day 02: Furniture cleaning, floor mop, vacuum cleaning, toilet deep cleaning
  • Day 03: Solution & equipment testing, toilet & glass cleaning, floor scrubbing & buffing
  • Days 04–06: On-the-job training
  • Day 07: Final practical test

Security Services Training

Security Officers & Supervisors undergo structured theoretical and practical training under the guidance of a full-time Training Manager. Site assignment is made only after successful completion of both on-site and center-based training.

We are a team of experts who take pride in delivering the highest standard of service. With years of experience in the industry, we’ve perfected our cleaning methods to ensure every job is done right.
