Questions

Frequently asked questions?

What types of services do you offer?

We specialize in a wide range of facility services including security, janitorial cleaning, pest control, catering, and total facility management tailored for both residential and commercial clients.

What makes your security services reliable?

Our security personnel are professionally trained under the supervision of retired defense officers. Each guard undergoes rigorous training, background verification, and on-site supervision to ensure top-tier protection.

Are your staff trained and certified?

Yes, all janitorial, security, and facility staff are trained in-house at our dedicated training center. We provide both theoretical and practical training to ensure our personnel are ready before site deployment.

How do I request a service from RANS?

You can contact us directly via phone or email to schedule a site visit, request a quotation, or set up a service plan. Our operations team will coordinate the process smoothly and professionally.

Do you provide emergency or one-time services?

Yes, we handle both ongoing contracts and one-time services such as deep cleaning, event-based cleaning, pest removal, and special occasions (weddings, office parties, etc.).

How do you ensure service quality?

We have a dedicated Quality Control team that performs regular inspections, collects client feedback, and grades performance. Additionally, our HSQE department ensures health, safety, and service standards are always met.

Do you offer customized packages?

Absolutely. We understand every client has unique needs. Whether you need daily janitorial support, part-time security, or seasonal pest control, we tailor our service packages accordingly.

We are a team of experts who take pride in delivering the highest standard of service. With years of experience in the industry, we’ve perfected our cleaning methods to ensure every job is done right.
