Catering Services

Food plays a crucial part in our lives. With our expertise and experienced chefs, we know how much healthy, fresh, and tasty food can influence work performance. Bearing that in mind, our competent chefs and in-house specialists take pride in creating nutritionally balanced and innovative menus.

This is our way of giving customers the best ingredients for a well-functioning and healthy workforce.

Our Catering Highlights

  • Fresh, healthy, and delicious meals prepared daily.
  • Innovative and customizable menus for diverse preferences.
  • Focus on boosting workplace wellness and energy.
  • Professional chefs and experienced food specialists.

Our Commitment to Quality Control

We provide customer comment cards to gather feedback regarding our catering services. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we continuously evolve based on your input.

We also have a dedicated quality control department that conducts detailed inspections, grades each site based on quality, and provides recommendations for improvements.

Health, Safety, and Environmental Commitment

RANS Facility Management is committed to providing professional services in compliance with country regulations. Our service delivery minimizes risks and ensures the safety of our staff, your staff, the public, and the environment.

We maintain quality and standards through:

  • Comprehensive auditing systems
  • Intensive staff training
  • Strict adherence to legal compliance
  • Continuous education and supervision

Our HSQE Department Activities

  • Safety Induction Training for New Employees
  • Carrying Out Site Risk Assessments
  • Onsite Health and Safety Training
  • Regular Site Inspections
  • Workers' Engagement Visits
  • Tool Box Talks and Awareness Programs
